Why Your Competitors Show Up on Google and You Don't

Jan 04, 2025 • 6 min read

← Back to Blog

You know your work is as good as theirs. Maybe better. So why do they show up on Google and you don't? Here are the real reasons.

They Have More Reviews

Check your competitor's Google reviews vs. yours. If they have 150 and you have 15, that's your answer. Start asking every customer for a review.

Their Profile is Complete

Look at their photos, services, posts, hours. If they're more complete than you, Google rewards them. Fill out every field.

They Have a Better Website

Compare sites. Do they have pages for each service? Do they mention your city? Is their site faster and mobile-friendly? Improve yours.

They've Been Around Longer

Google trusts older businesses. You can build authority faster with more reviews, content, and citations.

They Have More Citations

Check if they're listed places you're not. Get on all major directories with consistent information.

The Good News

None of this is magic. It's just work they did that you haven't done yet. The playing field is level.
