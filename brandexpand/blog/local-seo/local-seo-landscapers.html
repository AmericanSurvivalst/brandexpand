Local SEO for Landscapers

Jan 06, 2025 • 7 min read

← Back to Blog

Landscaping is visual. People want to see what you can do. Your online presence is crucial for being found and showing off your work.

The Landscaping Search Landscape

Searches peak in spring but continue year-round for maintenance, snow removal, hardscaping, and design consultations.

Google Business Profile for Landscapers

Choose "Landscaper" or "Lawn care service" as primary. Photos are huge - update them seasonally with spring flowers, summer lawns, fall cleanups, winter snow removal.

Keywords for Landscapers

Target "landscaper [city]," "lawn care," "lawn mowing service," "landscape design," "patio installation," "retaining wall," and seasonal services.

Visual Content is King

Invest in before/after photos, drone shots, time-lapse videos, and seasonal photos of the same properties. This works across all platforms.

Recurring Revenue Through SEO

Promote maintenance packages. Customers searching for lawn care often want ongoing service. Make it easy to sign up.
