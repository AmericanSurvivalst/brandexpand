How to Track Where Your Leads Come From

Nov 30, 2024 • 5 min read

← Back to Blog

If you don't know where leads come from, you can't make smart marketing decisions. Here's how to track everything.

Why Tracking Matters

You might be wasting half your marketing budget. Or you might be underspending on what works. Without tracking, you'll never know.

The Simple Method

Ask every single person who contacts you: "How did you find us?" Record the answer. Review monthly. This alone beats what most businesses do.

Call Tracking

Use different phone numbers for different sources. One for Google Ads, one for your website, one for yard signs. Services like CallRail make this easy.

Google Analytics

Shows where website visitors come from - organic search, ads, social media, direct. Set up conversion tracking to see which sources generate form submissions.

UTM Parameters

Add tags to URLs in your ads and posts. These show up in Google Analytics so you know exactly which ad or post drove traffic.

The Monthly Review

Every month, look at: leads by source, cost per lead by source, which sources convert to jobs. Double down on winners, cut losers.
