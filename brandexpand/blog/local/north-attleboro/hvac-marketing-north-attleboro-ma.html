HVAC Marketing in North Attleboro, MA: Get More Local Customers

Dec 04, 2024 • 8 min read

← Back to Blog

HVAC is a seasonal business, and in North Attleboro the swings are extreme. The contractors who thrive are the ones who generate leads consistently, not just during peak seasons.

The Real Money in HVAC

It's not emergency calls—it's system replacements and maintenance agreements. That's where your marketing should drive.

Google Business Profile Optimization

Primary category: "HVAC Contractor"

Secondary categories: "Air Conditioning Contractor," "Heating Contractor," "Furnace Repair Service"

Products: List brands you carry (Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Mitsubishi)

Photos: Weekly posts of installations, equipment, before/afters.

Reviews Are Critical

HVAC replacements are $8,000-15,000+ purchases. Customers research extensively. A company with 200+ positive reviews has a massive advantage.

High-value keywords: AC installation North Attleboro, furnace replacement North Attleboro, heat pump North Attleboro, HVAC maintenance North Attleboro, ductless mini-split North Attleboro

Seasonal Marketing Calendar

Spring: Push AC tune-ups hard. Early-bird pricing.

Summer: Emergency AC repair demand peaks. Optimize GBP for "AC repair."

Fall: Furnace tune-ups. Push maintenance agreements before winter.

Winter: Emergency furnace repairs. Market replacements to repeat-issue customers.

Maintenance Agreements: Your Recurring Revenue Engine

  • Predictable recurring revenue
  • First call when systems need replacing
  • Shoulder season work when emergency calls slow
  • Higher customer lifetime value

Make maintenance agreements prominent on your website. Offer them after every repair or installation.

The Bottom Line

Winning in North Attleboro's HVAC market requires year-round marketing. Dominate your GBP, build a review machine, push maintenance agreements, and time campaigns to match demand cycles.
