Door Hangers, Yard Signs, and Local Marketing That Works

Dec 10, 2024 • 6 min read

← Back to Blog

Not everything needs to be digital. Some old-school tactics work incredibly well for local businesses.

Yard Signs

Every job site is a billboard. Simple design - name, service, phone. Visible from street. Ask permission. Offer small discount to leave it up a week after.

Door Hangers

Just finished a job? Hit the neighbors while it's fresh. "We just completed work in your neighborhood" with an offer. Hit 20-30 houses closest to the job.

Vehicle Wraps

Your truck is advertising whether you like it or not. Phone number large enough to read from distance. Professional design. Keep vehicle clean.

Local Sponsorships

Little league teams, school events, charity events, local festivals. ROI isn't direct but builds community recognition over time.

Networking

Chamber of Commerce, BNI, trade associations. Build relationships that lead to referrals.

The Key

These tactics compound over time. The more visible you are in your community, the more people think of you when they need help.
