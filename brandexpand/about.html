The Short Version

Brand Expand exists because we got tired of watching small businesses get burned by agencies that overpromise and underdeliver. Fancy proposals, big retainers, and six months later you're wondering where the results are.

We've been in sales and marketing our whole careers. We know what it takes to actually grow a business, not just make pretty reports. So we started helping businesses do the stuff that actually moves the needle: websites that convert, content that ranks, ads that don't waste money.

"We'd rather have a tight roster of clients getting real results than a bloated list of clients getting excuses."

This isn't a side hustle. We're not farming your work out to random freelancers overseas. When you work with Brand Expand, you're working directly with us and a tight team that actually cares about your business.

How We Work

No nickel-and-diming for every little request. No disappearing for weeks and then hitting you with a generic "strategy deck." No overcomplicated proposals that leave you more confused than when you started.

We figure out what's actually going to help your business grow, then we do that. Simple. If something's not working, we adjust. If you have questions, you get answers, not a ticket number.

All of our clients stay past their initial 12 months because we walk the walk. Talk is cheap, so we measure everything and let the results speak for themselves.
