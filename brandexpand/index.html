FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you decide.

How long does a website take?

Most sites launch within 4 to 6 weeks. Complex e-commerce builds may take 8 to 10 weeks. We'll give you a timeline before we start.

What if I'm not happy with the design?

Every project includes revision rounds. Plus our 30-day guarantee means if you're not satisfied, you get your money back.

Do I own my website?

100%. You own everything: the design, content, and code. We build on our hosting to keep things simple, but you can take it with you anytime.

Can I cancel my monthly plan?

All plans include a 12 month agreement. After that, we ask for 30 days notice so we can wrap things up properly.

What's included in ad management?

Strategy, copy, targeting, optimization, and reporting. You provide the images or videos, we handle everything else.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely. Most of our clients are small to mid-size businesses looking to level up their online presence.
